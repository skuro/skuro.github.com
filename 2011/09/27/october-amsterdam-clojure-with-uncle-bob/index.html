October Amsterdam Clojure with Uncle Bob

September 27, 2011 -- filed under #clojure #meetup #amsterdam

I'm very excited to announce that we have finalized the details, and Wednesday, October 12th we're going to have a great Amsterdam Clojure meetup", with great talks and an awesome guest speaker: "Uncle" Bob Martin from Object Mentor and of Clean Coders fame.

Thanks to Sourcesense, which is once again backing up and ultimately allowing this event to happen in an awesome location such as the Pakhuis de Zwijger, I look forward to meet a number of Clojure enthusiast, newcomers or tired Java developers looking for something new.

We have limited seating possibilities, so make sure to register at the meetup official page to ensure there will be a spot for you!
